# Runner
A sonic runner game! use space to jump. You can see the high score as well. Used the Kaplay() library and react js to make this.
Link to play: https://runner-lake.vercel.app/

## How to play:
- Use space to jump!
- try to hit the enemies, this will add to your multiplier
- Also try to collect coins
- When game ends, you can see best score, and your current score, and a rating either A,B,C,D,E,F, or S based on your score
- press space to play again

AND MOST OF ALL, DON'T FORGET TO HAVE FUN! 🤩
